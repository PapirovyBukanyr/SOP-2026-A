	\noindent {\bfseries Optimization Homework 01:} 
	\noindent {\bfseries A}
	
	The Notip Table Company sells two models of its patented five-leg tables. 
	The basic version uses a wood top, requires 0.6 hour to assemble, and sells for a profit of \$200. 
	The deluxe model takes 1.5 hours to assemble because of its glass top, and sells for a profit of \$350. 
	Over the next week the company has 300 legs, 50 wood tops, 35 glass tops, and 63 hours of assembly available. 
	Notip wishes to determine a maximum profit production plan assuming that everything produced can be sold.
	
	\begin{enumerate}
		\item Analyze the problem. Use figures for explanations in a helpful way. 
		Introduce your notation that you will further use. Classify the problem and the model. 
		Find related literature references.
		
		\item Formulate a mathematical programming model with 4 main constraints to select an optimal production plan using decision variables 
		$x_1$ is a number of basic models produced and $x_2$ is a number of deluxe models. 
		At first introduce model with data then write model in general way using the initial notation.
		
		\item Using a 2-dimensional plot, solve your model graphically for an optimal product mix, and explain why it is unique.
		
		\item Discuss theoretical properties of the model (existence of the solution, convexity etc.).
		
		\item Solve your model with the Excel Solver.
		
		\item Enter and solve your model with the GAMS software.
		
		\item Solve your model with the simplex method.
		
		\item On a separate 2-dimensional plot, show that the model has alternative optimal solutions if profits are \$120 and \$300, respectively.
		
		\item Suggest nonlinear terms in the objective or in the constraints. Motivate them and explain their possible introduction.
		
		\item Identify possible random parameters and explain why they can be random by your opinion.
		
		\item Implement e.g.\ in Python with some optimization library.
	\end{enumerate}
